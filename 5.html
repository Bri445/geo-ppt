Major Pharmaceutical Hubs in India

  • Hyderabad: 'Vaccine Hub of India', home to over 200 pharma companies
  • Gujarat: Major manufacturing hub for bulk drugs and formulations
  • Mumbai-Pune: Corporate headquarters and R&D centers
  • Bengaluru: Biotech and innovative pharmaceutical research
  • Baddi (Himachal Pradesh): Tax incentives driving manufacturing growth
