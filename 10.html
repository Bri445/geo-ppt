Impact of Geography & Infrastructure on Pharma Distribution

  • Diverse Geography: Mountains in north, coastal areas, plains and plateaus affect transportation networks
  • Infrastructure Hubs: Major pharma clusters near ports (Mumbai, Vizag) and metro cities (Hyderabad, Delhi NCR)
  • Cold Chain: Critical for vaccines and biologics, concentrated in metro areas
  • Last-mile Challenges: Rural and remote areas face significant accessibility issues
Cold Chain Capacity
Over 90% of India's pharmaceutical cold storage infrastructure is concentrated in tier-1 and tier-2 cities, creating distribution challenges for temperature-sensitive medications in rural areas
Geography directly impacts logistics costs, which can represent 2-3% of total sales value for Indian pharmaceutical companies
