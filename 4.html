Evolution of India's Pharmaceutical Sector

  • 1940s: Early beginnings with establishment of first pharmaceutical companies
  • 1970: Patent Act passed allowing process patents, not product patents
  • 1990s-2000s: Liberalization and global expansion phase
  • 2005: Product patent regime implemented
  • Present: Third largest producer by volume, 20% of global generic exports
