Government Policies: PLI Scheme & Support

  • Production Linked Incentive (PLI) Scheme with ₹15,000 crore outlay
  • Focuses on domestic manufacturing of critical APIs and drug intermediates
  • Supports production of 41 specific pharmaceutical ingredients
  • Aims to reduce import dependency and increase self-reliance
  • Additional policy support through pharma clusters and tax incentives
