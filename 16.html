Conclusion & India's Vision

Key Achievements

  • Global Generic Leader: 20% of global generic exports, serving over 200 countries
  • Manufacturing Powerhouse: 3rd largest pharmaceutical producer by volume
  • Affordable Access: Critical role in providing low-cost medicines to developing nations
  • Strategic Hubs: Established pharmaceutical clusters creating specialized ecosystems

Vision 2030+

  • Market Growth: Expanding from $65 billion to $130 billion by 2030
  • Export Doubling: Increasing exports to $60-65 billion by 2030
  • Innovation Focus: Shifting from generics to complex drugs and biologics
  • Sustainable Production: Leading in green pharmaceutical manufacturing
